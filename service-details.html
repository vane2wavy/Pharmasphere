Drug Classification

The 4 Drug Classification

These are four drug classification

Prescription or Legend drugs

These are medications that require a prescription from licensed healthcare professionals such as physicians, dentists, or veterinarians.

Nonprescription or Over-the-counter drugs

These are medications that can be legally obtained without a prescription. They are considered safe for use by laypersons when used according to provided directions and for intended conditions.

Investigational drugs

These medications undergo clinical studies to assess their effectiveness in treating specific diseases.

Illicit or "street drugs"

These are drugs used and distributed illegally outside of medical supervision and regulation.
